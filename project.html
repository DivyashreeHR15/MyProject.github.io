MINI PROJECT


1] Detection Of Rice Leaf Disease Using Image Processing


        The spread of various diseases in plants has increased in recent years. There are variety of
        plant pathogens such as viral, bacterial, fungal and these can damage different plant parts
        above and below the ground. Identifying the correct disease symptoms and understanding when 
        to control these diseases are difficult. Detection of various diseases from images are one 
        of the interesting research area in agriculture sector. So nowadays the detection of diseases
        in different plant parts are very significant and relevant in our society. Image processing
        and machine learning provide variety of techniques for most of the steps in workflow 
        sequence to analyze image that can be effectively utilized for disease detection in plants.
        Techniques such as “Filtering, CLAHE algorithm etc.
